Pixle? What is that?

Pixle is a very short and casual game which challenges its players with different puzzles each day.
Puzzles are created out of a predefined pattern and randomly chosen emojis. Our pixle generator mixes things up and spits out interesting and challenging puzzles, which are just wanting to be solved. Their difficulty varies from being a three-by-three grid with just two emojis to being an up to five-by-five grid with five different emojis.
Games like Wordle and Picross inspired the development of Pixle. It combines different aspects and improves these and the overall gameplay. Besides solving puzzles on your own, you can challenge your friends and family to these nerve-braking puzzles!
Pixle is now available on the web and we challenge you to solve todays puzzle!

About puzzles and pixles

Puzzles, or pixles as we like to call them, are a fundamental part of the game Pixle. After all, the name "Pixle" derives from them.
But we don't want to talk all day about this boring theoretical stuff. How are these pixles created? What is the process?
We built a small generator, which gets fed a few different, predefined patterns and a list of emojis. It chooses one random pattern out of the bunch and checks its requirements, which in this case would be the amount of emojis needed to satisfy its needs. Once it knows the correct amount, it randomly picks various emojis out of the list and makes sure double usage won't happen. An emoji can only be used once in each pattern.
A pattern and its emojis have been chosen, now it is time to throw them together and save in an array. A new pixle is born! Along with the pixle, other important information is stored in the array, such as the pixle id and publication date.
And that should cover everything. Now you really know everything about the puzzles, pixles and the game Pixle.
