Pixle? What is that?

Pixle is a daily-puzzle game, inspired by nonograms and wordle.
Each day reveals a new puzzle to solve, which vary in difficulty.

Wondering how pixle works? No worries, we will explain everything thoroughly.
Below you can see a grid of symbols or as we call them: "emojis".
This is the area where the fun happens.

The grid displays a pattern which consists of up to five different emojis.
You are shown this pattern once and as soon as its hidden, you have to use your memory to rebuild the pattern.
Doesn't seem to difficult, right?

Everytime you place an emoji on the wrong tile, you lose a try on that specific tile.
Look at it as if it's the health of your player. As soon as you run out of lives, you lose and have to retry.
